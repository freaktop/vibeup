HOW TO INSTALL THE APK ON YOUR ANDROID DEVICE
==============================================

STEP 1: LOCATE THE APK FILE
----------------------------
The APK file is located at:
C:\Users\model\Documents\apps\mobile-web\android\app\build\outputs\apk\debug\app-debug.apk

STEP 2: TRANSFER TO YOUR PHONE
-------------------------------
Option A - USB Cable:
1. Connect your Android phone to your computer via USB cable
2. On your phone, when prompted, select "File Transfer" or "MTP" mode
3. Open File Explorer on your computer
4. Navigate to the folder above
5. Copy "app-debug.apk" file
6. Paste it to your phone (usually appears as a drive in File Explorer)
7. Paste into the "Downloads" folder on your phone

Option B - Email/Cloud:
1. Email the "app-debug.apk" file to yourself
2. Open the email on your phone
3. Download the attachment
4. The file will be in your Downloads folder

STEP 3: INSTALL ON YOUR PHONE
------------------------------
1. On your Android phone, open the "Files" or "File Manager" app
2. Navigate to the "Downloads" folder
3. Find "app-debug.apk" file
4. Tap on it
5. If you see a warning about "Unknown Sources":
   - Tap "Settings"
   - Turn ON "Allow from this source" or "Install unknown apps"
   - Go back and tap the APK again
6. Tap "Install"
7. Wait for installation to complete
8. Tap "Open" or find "VibeUp" in your app drawer

STEP 4: TEST THE APP
---------------------
Once installed, open VibeUp and you should see:
✓ Grid view in Discover tab (not swipe cards)
✓ 4 sub-tabs: Near Me, Right Now, Dating, Going Out
✓ More profiles (15 total)
✓ Profile detail modal when tapping profiles
✓ Loading states
✓ Better error messages

TROUBLESHOOTING ADB (If you want automatic install)
----------------------------------------------------
If you want adb to work for automatic installs:

1. On your phone: Settings > About Phone
2. Tap "Build Number" 7 times (enables Developer Options)
3. Go back to Settings > Developer Options
4. Turn ON "USB Debugging"
5. Connect phone via USB
6. On phone, when popup appears: "Allow USB debugging?" → Tap ALLOW
7. Check "Always allow from this computer" if prompted
8. Run: adb devices (should show your device)

Then I can install automatically with:
adb install -r app-debug.apk
